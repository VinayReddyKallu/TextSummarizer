About Us:

We are a team of final year undergraduate students in the Computer Science and Engineering department at SNIST, Hyderabad.
During the beginning of our final year, we had teamed up for the Major Project. And right from that moment, we started facing a challenge in deciding the topic for our project. We browsed through many websites where we would read lots and lots of content only to realise that we actually got a very little information from that content. As it is said, necessity is the mother of invention, from the challenge we faced in going through those long articles and still getting very little useful information out of it, came the idea of what you see now, "Text Summarizer", a tool that can summarize the long articles, texts, or paragraphs and give only the useful content from those. We put in a lot of effort to make our idea, a reality, and are continuously working on it, to make it better and better. We hope our text summarizer is going to be of great use for you.
